Research Interest

My research area is experimental condensed matter physics, primarily focused on growing, fabricating and characterizing different materials to investigate their physical properties with transport measurement. The focused subjects are mainly including magnetic materials, topological materials, 2D van der Waals materials. The ultimate goal is to achieve rational design of quantum materials towards properties on demand.
