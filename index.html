Introduction

I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Physics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I am dedicated to research in condensed matter physics and teaching CPHO.

As an undergraduate student, I led physics students to create the Physics Student Club and served as External Vice President in it. My research interests focus on the characterization and properties of topological materials, and I have conducted relevant research training in related groups, mainly in experimental physics. At the same time, I am studying computational materials science on my own, and I am also conducting research in computational physics. I work part-time teaching physics competitions for Harbin Normal University High School and Shenzhen Senior High School.

Academic Interests

  • Measurement and data analysis of physical properties of two-dimensional or topological materials using PPMS (Physical Property Measurement System).
  • Manipulate the growth of single crystal materials as well as two-dimensional materials including, but not limited to, MBE, chemical synthesis methods.
  • Data analysis, quantitative analysis and data visualization, including experimental platform building/financial model development, etc.
  • CPHO teaching, part-time teaching physics competitions in secondary schools.
